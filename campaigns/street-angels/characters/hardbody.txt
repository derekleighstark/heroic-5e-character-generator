### 🛡️ How to Play Hardbody: A New Player's Guide

Welcome to *HEROIC 5e*! Hardbody is a **Guardian**. While Strikers and Vanguards dart around dealing massive damage, you are the team's anchor. You win fights by wading into the middle of the chaos, soaking up hits that would kill your teammates, and bullying the biggest villains on the battlefield. 

Here is a simple breakdown of how your character operates during combat.

#### 💥 Bread-and-Butter Combos

**Combo 1: The Human Shield (Ultimate Defense)**
*Use this when the villain tries to target your squishiest teammate.*
1.  **The Trigger:** The GM announces that an enemy has successfully hit your ally.
2.  **Reaction (Stand Between):** Because you are a Guardian, you immediately use your Reaction to intercept the attack. The ally takes no damage, and *you* become the target.
3.  **Reaction (Brace for Impact):** At the exact same time, you trigger your Super Durability power. You roll `1d8 + 4` and subtract that number from the damage you are taking. 
4.  **The Result:** Between your *Brace for Impact* roll and your passive flat damage reduction (you reduce *all* incoming attacks by an additional 2 points automatically), you will likely reduce a massive, lethal blow to a tiny scratch. 

**Combo 2: The Bulldozer (Crowd Control)**
*Use this when enemies are clumped together or blocking a doorway.*
1.  **Action (Unstoppable Advance):** You charge straight through the battlefield. This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks, and you ignore difficult terrain. 
2.  **The Effect:** Any enemy in your path has to roll a Strength Save against your massive Effect Value (16). If they fail, they are violently shoved out of your way. 
3.  **Follow-Up:** You are now perfectly positioned right next to the enemy boss. Next turn, you can hit them with a **Mighty Blow** (`1d8 + 3` damage, and knocking them Prone on a Strong Success).

---

#### 🛡️ Core Mechanics & Quick Tips

*   **You Start Fights with Extra Health:** At the beginning of *every* encounter, you automatically gain **4 Temporary Hit Points** from your Super Durability. These absorb damage before your real Hit Points are touched.
*   **You Shrug Off Pain:** You have a passive damage reduction of 2. Every single time you take damage from a punch, a bullet, or a fire blast, subtract 2 from it. It doesn't cost an action; it's just how tough your skin is. 
*   **The "Endure" Safety Net:** If a villain gets lucky and somehow reduces your 31 HP to 0, you don't go down. Your Guardian *Endure* class feature automatically kicks in, dropping you to 1 HP instead and keeping you in the fight. 
*   **Using Your Edge:** You have the **Redeemer** Calling. You start the session with 3 Edge points (which let you reroll dice or push your powers). You earn Edge back by roleplaying your past—like confronting old gang members from your "Enemy" Flaw without running away, or showing mercy to a street kid who reminds you of who you used to be!
