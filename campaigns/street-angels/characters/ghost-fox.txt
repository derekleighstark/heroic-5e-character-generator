### 🥋 How to Play Ghost Fox: A New Player's Guide

Welcome to *HEROIC 5e*! Ghost Fox is a **Striker**. You don't win fights by taking a lot of hits; you win by picking your targets carefully, setting up tactical advantages, and hitting enemies where it hurts the most.

Here is a simple breakdown of how your character operates during combat. 

#### Your Action Economy (What you can do on your Turn)
Every Turn, you get to move (up to 30 feet) and take **three** types of actions:
1.  **An Action:** Your main attack (like *Brutal Strike* or *Gadget Strike*).
2.  **A Bonus Action:** A quick secondary move (like firing your *Grapnel Line* or using your *Exploit Opening* feature).
3.  **A Reaction:** Used when it isn't your turn to defend yourself or seize an opportunity. 

---

#### 💥 Bread-and-Butter Combos

**Combo 1: The Striker’s Lockdown (Maximum Damage)**
*The ultimate 1-on-1 takedown against a tough enemy.*
1.  **Action:** Use **Brutal Strike** on an enemy. You roll `1d20 + 6` against their Parry/Block defense. Because of your *Precision Strike*, you always hit incredibly hard (dealing `1d8 + 8` damage!). 
2.  **The Trigger:** If you beat their defense by 5 or more (a "Strong Success"), you get a Momentum Effect. Choose to **knock them Prone**.
3.  **Bonus Action:** Because they are now Prone (a Condition), you can immediately use your Striker class feature, **Exploit Opening**. You deal an extra `1d8` of damage *and* force them to make a Save. If they fail, they are **Dazed** (meaning they lose actions on their next turn).
4.  **The Follow-up:** Next turn, because they are still Prone/Dazed, your **Takedown Artist** talent automatically adds +2 to your damage against them!.

**Combo 2: The Smoke Ambush (Crowd Control)**
*Perfect for when you are outnumbered or need to protect your team.*
1.  **Free Action:** Throw your **Smoke Pellet** (thanks to your *Quick Deploy* enhancement, this doesn't cost an action!). A 15-foot area is now covered in thick smoke. 
2.  **Movement:** Because it's *your* smoke, you can see and move through it easily. Enemies shooting at you or your allies from inside the smoke have Disadvantage on their attacks.
3.  **Action:** Throw a **Gadget Strike** (`1d20 + 3`) from the edge of the smoke at a ranged enemy to soften them up.
4.  **Bonus Action:** Use your **Grapnel Line** to immediately swing up to a rooftop or out of danger, leaving the enemies coughing in the dark.

---

#### 🛡️ Defensive Moves & Quick Tips

*   **You Are Hard to Hit:** Because of your *Martial Mastery* and *Precise Form* abilities, your **Parry/Block defense is +9** and your **Dodge is +7**. When an enemy attacks you, the Game Master rolls and *they* have to beat those high numbers!
*   **You Always Go First:** Your *Combat Awareness* power means you can **never be surprised** by an ambush, and you roll your Initiative with Advantage (roll 2d20 and take the highest). 
*   **Use Your Edge:** You start every game session with 3 **Edge** points. If you miss a crucial attack or fail a save, spend 1 Edge to reroll the dice. You earn Edge back by roleplaying your **Avenger Calling**—like tracking down Syndicate members or putting yourself at risk to solve a mystery.
