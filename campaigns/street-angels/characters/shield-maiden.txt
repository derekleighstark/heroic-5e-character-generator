### 🛡️ How to Play Shield Maiden: A New Player's Guide

Welcome to *HEROIC 5e*! Shield Maiden is a **Bruiser**, meaning she hits with the force of a collapsing building. You do not win fights through stealth or fancy gadgets; you win by controlling the battlefield, throwing cars, knocking multiple enemies down at once, and smashing through whatever gets in your way. 

Here is a simple breakdown of how your character operates during combat.

#### 💥 Bread-and-Butter Combos

**Combo 1: The Shield Slam (Crowd Control)**
*Use this when enemies group up together.*
1.  **Action (Shockwave Strike):** You slam your Aegis into the ground. Every enemy in a 15-foot radius takes `1d8+6` damage and has to make a Strength save against your Effect Value of 15. If they fail, they are knocked Prone.
2.  **The Follow-Up (Shockwave Mastery):** Because you took the *Shockwave Mastery* enhancement, any enemy you knock Prone has to instantly make a *second* save (Constitution). If they fail that, they are also **Dazed** (meaning they lose their Action or Bonus Action next turn).
3.  **The Cleanup:** Next turn, walk up to the Prone, Dazed enemies and hit them with *Mighty Blow*. Because they are Prone/Dazed, your **Takedown Artist** talent automatically adds +2 to your damage!

**Combo 2: The Linebreaker (Maximum Single Target)**
*Use this to completely obliterate a tough villain.*
1.  **Action (Mighty Blow):** You bash the villain with your shield. You roll `1d20+5` against their Parry/Block defense. If you hit, you deal `1d8+6` damage. If you beat their defense by 5 or more (a Strong Success), you can automatically push them 25 feet or knock them Prone.
2.  **Bonus Action (Heavy Impact):** Because you landed a hit, immediately use your Bruiser *Heavy Impact* class feature. You force them to make a Strength save. If they fail, you can Daze them, push them even further, or knock them Prone!
3.  **The Finisher (Chain Strike):** If this brutal combo reduces the enemy to 0 HP, your *Chain Strike* enhancement immediately triggers, giving you a completely free *Mighty Blow* attack against a second enemy standing nearby!

#### 🛡️ Core Mechanics & Quick Tips

*   **You Always Hit Hard:** Because you are a Bruiser, your *Heavy Hitter* feature adds your +3 Strength modifier to **all** damage rolls. That means even your basic *Object Throw* and your *Shockwave Strike* deal massive bonus damage. 
*   **Don't Lose Your Shield:** Your Aegis of Vēlā gives you all your Super Strength powers. It has the *Gear Dependent* Limitation. If a villain disarms you or suppresses the shield's magic, you lose your strength until you get it back. Keep it close!
*   **You Can Save Lives Instantly:** Your *Stabilize* utility power is a game-changer. If an ally or an innocent civilian drops to 0 HP and is bleeding out, you don't need to waste your Turn doing CPR. As a quick Bonus Action, you can touch them with your shield and instantly stabilize them, keeping them alive while still making your attacks!
*   **Using Your Edge:** You have the **Protector** Calling. You start the session with 3 Edge points (which let you reroll dice or push your powers). You earn Edge back by roleplaying your Calling—like placing yourself between danger and someone who cannot defend themselves, or refusing to retreat from a losing fight because someone still needs you to protect them!
