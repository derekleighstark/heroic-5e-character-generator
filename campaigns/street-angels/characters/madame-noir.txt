### 🔮 How to Play Madame Noir: A New Player's Guide

Welcome to *HEROIC 5e*! Madame Noir is a **Strategist**, meaning you do not win fights by dealing the most damage or taking the most hits. You are the brains of the operation. You win by manipulating the battlefield, uncovering the enemy's secrets before they even act, and setting up your teammates for massive, devastating attacks.

Here is a simple breakdown of how your character operates during combat and investigations.

#### 💥 Bread-and-Butter Combos

**Combo 1: The Mastermind's Trap (Defense into Offense)**
*Use this when an enemy thinks you are an easy target.*
1.  **Bonus Action:** Use your Strategist class feature, **Tactical Analysis**, to mark a villain. You instantly ask the GM for their hidden vulnerability or their highest attack stat.
2.  **Action:** Fire your handgun at the analyzed target. If you hit, you trigger your **Tactical Exploitation** feature, allowing you to deal an extra 3 damage, or give your heavy-hitting ally Advantage on their next attack!
3.  **The Trap (Reaction):** When the angry villain tries to attack you back, you use your **Forewarned** power to instantly add +3 to your Active Defense. 
4.  **The Counter (Reaction):** Because you dodged the attack, you immediately trigger your **Precognitive Counter** power. You get a free attack against them with Advantage. If it hits, they take extra damage and are **Shaken** (giving them Disadvantage on their next roll)!

**Combo 2: The Unseen Observer (Social / Investigation)**
*Use this to completely unwrap a mystery without throwing a single punch.*
1.  **The Setup:** When you walk into a fancy gala or a mob meeting, spend 1 minute quietly observing the VIP using your **Cold Reader** talent. You gain Advantage on your next social check and learn their deepest fear or desire.
2.  **The Reveal:** Trigger your **Read the Room** utility power. You get to ask the GM two massive questions, such as *"What is the most significant hidden danger here?"*
3.  **The Climax:** If the answers point to someone who was recently murdered, use your **Spirit Speech** power to briefly talk to their ghost and see their final moments. You just solved the mystery before the police even secured the crime scene!

#### 🛡️ Core Mechanics & Quick Tips

*   **You Are a Social God:** With a **+8 Social Active Defense**, it is mathematically incredibly difficult for villains to intimidate, manipulate, or lie to you. 
*   **You Are Never Surprised:** Because of your *Precognition* power, it is impossible for enemies to ambush you. You always roll Initiative with Advantage, meaning you will almost always act first to set up your team.
*   **Using Your Edge:** You have the **Investigator** Calling. You start the session with 3 Edge points (which let you reroll dice or trigger team combos). You earn Edge back by roleplaying your Calling—like refusing to walk away from a dangerous clue, or prioritizing saving an innocent civilian even if it means the suspect you are investigating gets away!
