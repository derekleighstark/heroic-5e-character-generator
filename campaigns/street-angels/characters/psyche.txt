### 🧠 How to Play Psyche: A New Player's Guide

Welcome to *HEROIC 5e*! Psyche is a **Sentinel** who manipulates the battlefield using an incredibly versatile mix of Telekinesis, Telepathy, and Illusion. You are not a frontline brawler; your physical Fighting is low, but your mind is a weapon. You win fights by keeping enemies at a distance, confusing their senses, and feeding critical information to your team.

Here is a simple breakdown of how your character operates during combat and investigations.

#### 💥 Bread-and-Butter Combos

**Combo 1: The Phantom Controller (Defense into Offense)**
*Use this to lock down an enemy while keeping yourself perfectly safe.*
1.  **Action 1 (False Image):** Create an illusionary duplicate of yourself. Enemies who shoot at you won't know which is the real Psyche, giving them Disadvantage on their attacks!
2.  **Next Turn Action (Phantom Strike):** Blast a tough enemy's mind. You roll against their *Willpower* (which is usually much lower than their physical Dodge!). If you hit, they take psychic damage and become *Shaken* (they suffer Disadvantage on their next roll).
3.  **The Follow-Up (Telekinetic Shove):** Because they are Shaken, use your *Power Focus* talent on your Telekinetic Shove to give them Disadvantage on their save. You blast them with invisible force, dealing damage and hurling them 25-50 feet backward or knocking them Prone!

**Combo 2: The Unseen Overwatch (Tactical Command)**
*Psyche's greatest strength is the information she gathers before the first punch is thrown.*
1.  **The Setup (Threat Assessment):** At the absolute start of the fight, the GM *has* to tell you who the strongest enemy is, who deals the most damage, and if they have any hidden traps or objectives.
2.  **Action (Telepathic Link):** Link the minds of your heaviest hitters (like Bruisers or Vanguards). 
3.  **Bonus Action (Surface Read):** Look into the mind of the enemy boss to steal their immediate intention.
4.  **Free Action:** Relay everything you just learned silently into your team's minds. You just solved the encounter puzzle without making a sound.

---

#### 🛡️ Defensive Moves & Quick Tips

*   **You Are Never Ambushed:** Thanks to your *Reactive Awareness* Sentinel feature, you cannot be surprised. You also roll your Initiative with Advantage (rolling 2d20 and taking the higher result), meaning you almost always act first.
*   **You are Hard to Hit:** With a 16 Dexterity and a 16 Perception, your **Dodge Active Defense is an astonishing +8** right out of the gate. Ranged attackers will struggle to hit you. Similarly, your **Willpower Active Defense is a +7**, making your mind an absolute fortress against enemy telepaths.
*   **Adaptive Biology is a Lifesaver:** Once per encounter when you use your Telekinetic Shove, your Enhanced Origin kicks in. You can choose to instantly gain 2 Temporary Hit Points just to buffer yourself against an incoming attack!
*   **Using Your Edge:** You have the **Chaos & Connection** Calling. You earn your Edge points by prioritizing emotional connections and understanding over cold tactical efficiency—even if that means reaching out telepathically to a villain or making a dangerous choice because of love or loyalty to a teammate!
