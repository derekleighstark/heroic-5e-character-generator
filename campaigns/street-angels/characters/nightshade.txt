*   **Origin Talent (Monster):** *Apex Predator.* Once per encounter, when you move toward a target aggressively to close distance, you make that movement without provoking Reactions. If you reach the target and attack on the same Turn, you gain Advantage on that attack.

Once per encounter as a Reaction, you may reroll a failed save against one of those conditions. When you use Second Wind or Steady On, you gain temporary HP equal to WIS (+3).
*   **Class Feature (Warden):** *Force of Will.* Once per Turn as a Bonus Action, when a creature within Short range (25-50 ft) causes Shaken, Frightened, Dazed, or Charmed, you may project your discipline against them. ### 🦇 How to Play Nightshade: A New Player's Guide

Welcome to *HEROIC 5e*! Nightshade is a **Warden** with a supernatural twist. While Strikers deal massive damage and Guardians soak up physical hits, Wardens are the unshakable anchors of the team's willpower. You win fights by completely locking down the enemy's psychological advantages, shrugging off mental attacks, and using the shadows to terrify criminals into submission.

Here is a simple breakdown of how your character operates during combat and investigations.

#### 💥 Bread-and-Butter Combos

**Combo 1: The Predator from the Dark (Hit and Fade)**
*Use this to deal debilitating damage while remaining completely untouchable.*
1.  **Free Action:** Because you are standing in dim light, use your **Shadow Hide** utility to vanish. You gain the *Hidden* condition.
2.  **The Approach (Apex Predator):** Trigger your Origin talent. You close the distance on an enemy aggressively without provoking any opportunity attacks. 
3.  **Action:** Lash out from the darkness with **Shadow Lash**. Because you were *Hidden* and used *Apex Predator*, you attack with Advantage! If you roll a Strong Success, your *Resonant Shadow* enhancement kicks in: the enemy takes `1d8+3` damage, and is instantly **Shaken and Blinded**. 
4.  **Bonus Action:** Use **Dim Light Step** to immediately teleport 25 feet away into another shadow, ready to strike again next turn while the enemy flails blindly.

**Combo 2: The Shape of Nightmares (Crowd Control)**
*Use this when outnumbered or when a villain tries to rally their thugs.*
1.  **Action:** Unleash your **Fear Shape** encounter power. Thanks to your *Extended Fear Shape* enhancement, you can target two enemies at once. You roll `1d20+5` against their Willpower.
2.  **The Save:** If you hit, they must roll against your massive Effect Value of 15. If they fail, they are completely **Frightened** of you—meaning they have Disadvantage on all rolls and cannot willingly move closer to you.
3.  **Bonus Action:** If an enemy tries to use a power to charm or frighten one of your teammates, immediately use your Warden **Force of Will** to project your own discipline against them, instantly stripping away their effect and leaving *them* Shaken instead!

---

#### 🛡️ Core Mechanics & Quick Tips

*   **You Own the Shadows:** Because of *Shadow Control 1* and your *Inhuman Body*, you can see perfectly in absolute darkness (even magical darkness). Your enemies cannot. Always try to fight in alleys, unlit warehouses, or at night. 
*   **Your Mind is a Fortress:** Between your *Inner Fortress* class feature and your *Iron Will* talent, it is mathematically almost impossible for an enemy to frighten, charm, or daze you. If a vampire tries to mind-control you, you roll your save with Advantage, and even if you somehow fail, you can use your Reaction to reroll it!
*   **You Speak for the Dead:** As a supernatural investigator, your **Spirit Speech** utility power is a game-changer. If you find a murder victim, you don't need to dust for fingerprints; you can just ask their ghost who killed them and literally see their final moments through their eyes!
*   **Using Your Edge:** You have the **Redeemer** Calling, meaning you are trying to make up for your monstrous nature by saving others. You earn your Edge points by extending mercy to villains who remind you of what you used to be, or by standing your ground and confronting your dark past without running away.
