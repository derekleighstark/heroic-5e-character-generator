### 🦅 How to Play Starling: A New Player's Guide

Welcome to *HEROIC 5e*! Starling is a **Vanguard**. While Bruisers want to stand still and soak up hits, you survive through sheer, unstoppable momentum. You win fights by launching into the air, raining down blasts from above, dive-bombing crucial targets, and making yourself incredibly difficult for villains to pin down.

Here is a simple breakdown of how your character operates during combat.

#### 💥 Bread-and-Butter Combos

**Combo 1: The Hit-and-Run (Maximum Single Target)**
*Use this to obliterate an enemy while protecting yourself from retaliation.*
1.  **The Approach:** Use your **Flight** to soar 25 feet through the air toward your target. 
2.  **The Trigger (Elusive):** Because you are a Vanguard and moved more than 15 feet, your **Elusive** class feature instantly activates. The next enemy who tries to attack you will have Disadvantage!
3.  **Action (Sonic Dive):** Because you moved 25 feet, you meet the prerequisite to use your **Sonic Dive** At-Will power. You slam into the enemy from the sky! You roll `1d20+6` against their Parry/Block. If you hit, you deal a massive `2d8+4` damage, and if you roll a Strong Success, you knock them flat on their back (Prone).

**Combo 2: The Sonic Boom (Crowd Control & Team Defense)**
*Use this when enemies group up, or when your team is in trouble.*
1.  **Free Action (Speed Burst):** Trigger your *Speed Burst* enhancement to double your flight speed to 60 feet for the turn. Fly straight up into the air so you have a perfect vantage point over the battlefield. 
2.  **Action (Sonic Burst):** Unleash your encounter power. You target a 40-foot wide circle (20-ft radius) on the ground, rolling your attack against *every single enemy* caught inside it, dealing `1d8+4` sonic damage to anyone you hit.
3.  **Reaction (Sonic Shield):** When the angry villains shoot back at your teammates, you use your Reaction to trigger your **Sonic Shield**. Because you took the *Shield Ally* enhancement, you can project a sonic barrier over an ally up to 50 feet away, reducing the damage they take by `1d8+2`!

---

#### 🛡️ Core Mechanics & Quick Tips

*   **Never Stop Moving:** Vanguards die when they stand still. If you don't move at least 15 feet every turn, you lose your *Elusive* defensive buff. Always keep flying, repositioning, and making the enemy chase you.
*   **Push Your Powers:** Don't forget your Enhanced Origin! Once per fight, when you use *Sonic Blast* or *Sonic Dive*, you can trigger **Adaptive Biology** to instantly gain 2 Temporary Hit Points, giving yourself a quick buffer against enemy attacks. 
*   **Sonar Vision:** Your *Environmental Interaction* utility power essentially gives you Daredevil's radar sense. If a villain turns out the lights or drops a smoke bomb, use your sonar navigation to "see" the battlefield using sound waves!
*   **Using Your Edge:** You have the **Protector** Calling. You earn your Edge points by making sacrifices for your protected circle—like breaking off an attack against the main villain just to swoop down and shield an innocent bystander from falling debris!
